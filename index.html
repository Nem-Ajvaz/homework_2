About Me

I graduated from RMIT with a Bachelor of Engineering (Computing and Networking) in 2017. Since then I have been employed by Mondo, who focus on spacial enterprise software, first as a Service Desk Technician and now a Service Desk Team Lead / psuedo Scrum Master with a development team.

My goal for the last 1-2 years has been to get a full time job as a Scrum Master / Agile Coach. I joined University of Sydneys Web Development Bootcamp to become qualified as a developer and work in the industry for a few years to gain a more intimate knowledge of a developers day to day life which will help me in the future as a Scrum Master as well as teach me technical skills.

In my spare time I enjoy playing chess, long distance cycling, reading and amateur photography.
